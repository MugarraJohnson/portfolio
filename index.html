About me

I work at the intersection of predictive modelling, impact evaluation, research and development. Over the last 6 years , I've designed and deployed production-grade ML systems, automated ETL pipelines, and interactive dashboards used by stakeholders including government ministries (MAAIF), and international NGOs across East Africa.

At MAAIF / Makerere University, I developed prediction models for the Vanilla value chain that were cognizant of a climate-change while integrating locally available data and web scrapping of NASA POWER satellite data — achieving an 30 % improvement in forecast accuracy and presenting findings to the stakeholders in public and private sectors including the State Minister of Agriculture.
At USAID SITES (DLH Corp & SSS Inc.) I conducted impact evaluations efficiently, ensuring that findings were presented for validation workshops within record times.

I hold an M.Sc. in Data Science from MAHE India and am google certified in PMP, Power BI, SAS Statistics, and Tableau.
Based in Kampala and I am available for remote and international roles.

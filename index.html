About me

Turning data into development impact

I'm a Data Scientist who works at the intersection of machine learning, impact evaluation, and development policy. Over six years I've designed and deployed production-grade ML systems, automated ETL pipelines, and interactive dashboards used by government ministries, USAID programmes, and international NGOs across East Africa.

At MAAIF / Makerere University, I engineered a climate-resilient crop yield forecasting system powered by NASA POWER satellite data — achieving an 18% improvement in forecast accuracy and presenting findings directly to H.E. President Museveni. At USAID SITES (DLH Corp & SSS Inc.) I automated impact evaluation pipelines that compressed six-week analysis cycles down to under 10 days across 12 concurrent initiatives.

I hold an M.Sc. in Data Science from MAHE India and am certified in PMP, Power BI, SAS Statistics, and Tableau. Based in Kampala — available for remote and international roles.
